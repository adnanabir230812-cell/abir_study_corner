## Slide 3

What is biological control?

First coined by Harry Smith in relation to the biological control of insects

Suppression of insect populations by native or introduced enemies

Generic terms

A population-leveling process in which the population of one species lowers the number of another

## Slide 9

Definition:

An antagonist is the organism that suppresses or inhibits the growth or activity of a pest or pathogen

through various mechanisms like

competition, parasitism, or production of harmful substances

.

Trichoderma

harzianum

is a fungal antagonist that suppresses the growth of plant-pathogenic fungi like
